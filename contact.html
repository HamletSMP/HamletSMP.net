Frequently Asked Questions

What's the fastest way to get support?

Discord is the fastest option. Join our server and ask in the #support channel. Our team and community members are usually online and can help immediately.

I submitted an application. When will I hear back?

Application reviews take 3-5 business days. If you haven't heard back after 5 days, please message us on Discord. Do not email about application status - use Discord for faster response.

I found a bug. How do I report it?

For bugs and technical issues:

  1. Join our Discord and use #bug-reports
  2. Include: Minecraft version, what happened, steps to reproduce
  3. If possible, include screenshots or videos
Do you accept partnership or collaboration requests?

Yes! For partnership, sponsorship, or collaboration inquiries, please email hamletsmp@gmail.com with "Partnership Inquiry" in the subject line. Include details about your proposal.
